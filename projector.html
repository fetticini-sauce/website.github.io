Epson EX7240

Wireless Projector

When it's time to make a big presentation, you can depend on the performance of the Epson EX7240 Pro WXGA 3LCD projector. Wireless connection to your laptop or mobile device lets you focus on sharing your best ideas.

  • Epson EX7240 Pro wireless WXGA 3LCD projector weighs only 5.29 lb for easy transport.
  • This EX7240 Pro WXGA projector features 3LCD, 3-chip technology that provides high-performance presentations.
  • Simple Setup Suite allows for quick and easy positioning and setup.
  • 1280 x 800 WXGA offers better resolution than SVGA for full HD 1080p content.
  • Bright images and noticeable contrast. Up to 3,200 lumens of color brightness and 3,000 lumens of white brightness. 15,000:1 contrast ratio. Provides excellent color saturation.
  • Up to 10,000 hours of lamp life.
  • HDMI connectivity for vivid image quality and high-definition audio output all in 1 cable.
  • Remote control included for easier operation.
